---
title: SuperH CPU 7291
description: Unofficial documentation of the SuperH CPU 7291
---

The CPU7291 is used by the Pocket Viewer (PV) S1600, that's the earliest ancestor of the ClassPad series. Some of its codebase is still used in the ClassPad 400. 

## CPU7291 Opcode Formats

|---|---|---|
| `add Rm,Rn` | Adds Rm to Rn and stores result in Rn. | `0011nnnnmmmm1100` |
